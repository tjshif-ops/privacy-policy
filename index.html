Audio Video Converter

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Audio Video Converter ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the App.

1. Overview

Audio Video Converter is a local media utility app that allows users to compress video, convert media formats, trim clips, extract audio, convert audio, and manage exported files.

The App is designed so that media processing happens on your device. We do not upload your audio or video files to our servers for processing.

2. Information We Access

The App may access the following information only as needed to provide its features:

  • Audio and video files that you explicitly choose in the App
  • Basic file metadata such as file name, duration, size, and format
  • Locally generated export files and temporary processing files
  • Purchase status information from Google Play if you choose to buy Pro features

3. How We Use Information

The App uses accessed information only to provide app functionality, including:

  • Compressing, converting, trimming, or extracting media files
  • Saving export files to your device
  • Displaying export history and file paths
  • Restoring or validating Pro purchase access through Google Play
  • Managing temporary files stored on your device

4. Media Files and Local Processing

Your selected media files are processed locally on your device. We do not transmit your media files to our servers.

Exported files may be written to device media locations such as system Movies or Music folders, depending on file type and Android storage behavior.

Temporary files may be created in local app cache directories during processing. You can remove these files using the App’s cleanup tools or by uninstalling the App.

5. Purchases

If you choose to purchase Pro features, purchases are handled by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

We may read purchase entitlement information from Google Play in order to:

  • Unlock Pro features
  • Restore previous purchases
  • Confirm whether your account has active access to purchased functionality

6. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your media files with our own servers.

The App may interact with the following third parties only when required for core features:

  • Google Play, for purchase processing and purchase restoration
  • Android system components, for file picking, media storage, and file sharing actions you initiate

7. Advertising and Analytics

The App does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

At the time of this policy, the App is not designed to upload your media files for analytics or cloud processing.

8. Data Retention

Media files selected for processing remain on your device unless you delete them. Exported files remain on your device until you remove them.

Temporary files may remain on your device until they are cleaned by the App, removed by the system, or deleted by you.

9. Security

Because processing is primarily local, the main responsibility for device-level security depends on your device settings, operating system protections, and account controls.

We recommend that you keep your device updated and use standard device security protections.

10. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed specifically to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own servers.

11. International Users

If you use the App outside your home country, local laws may apply to your use of device storage, app purchases, and personal data protections.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
